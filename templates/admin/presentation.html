The Problem & Our Solution
Why Cambodia needs AI-assisted X-ray screening
{# Problem column #}
The Problem
Global Burden
Pneumonia kills 2.5 million people per year — the leading infectious disease cause of death worldwide.
Cambodia Challenge
≈ 1 radiologist per 50,000 patients. Rural provinces have virtually no diagnostic imaging services.
Diagnosis Delay
Average 3–7 day wait for chest X-ray review. Delayed diagnosis → worse outcomes, higher mortality.
{# Solution column #}
Our Solution
AI Screening in <2 seconds
CNN+ANN hybrid model achieves 92.6% accuracy. Results instantly. No radiologist needed for initial triage.
Accessible Anywhere
Web-based — works on any device with a browser. No app install. Upload X-ray, get result immediately.
Doctor Marketplace
{{ stats.approved_doctors }} verified doctors available for follow-up video consultation. Book + pay in 60 seconds.

Platform Actors & Permissions
Three roles — each with distinct capabilities and access levels
{# Admin #}
Admin
Full system control
  • Configure system & Telegram alerts
  • Approve or reject doctor accounts
  • Monitor all scans, logs & alerts
  • Manage subscriptions & ad placements
  • View revenue & system-flow analytics
{# Doctor #}
Doctor
Must be approved by Admin first
  • Self-register with license & credentials
  • Appear in patient marketplace (after approval)
  • View today's appointment schedule
  • Access patient scan + AI result pre-consult
  • Mark consultations complete, earn fees
{# Patient #}
Patient
Free or Pro tier
  • Upload X-ray (3/day free, unlimited Pro)

  • Browse & book verified doctors
  • Pay via ABA KHQR, join video consult
